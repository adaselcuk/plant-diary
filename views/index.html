Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is Foliage?

Foliage is a plant care app to log care activities, set reminders, ask FoliageAI for help, and have a community to support your questions. We also provide unique features such as providing plant health analytics, weather or location integration, and explaining eco-friendly practices.

How do I sign up?

Click the Sign Up button at the top of the page and fill out the form with your information. You will receive an email to verify your account and then you can start using Foliage!

Is Foliage free?

Yes! The website is completely free.

Account and Profile

How do I change my password?

Click on your profile icon in the top right corner of the screen and select Change Password. You will be prompted to enter your current password and then your new password.

How do I delete my account?

Click on your profile icon in the top right corner of the screen and select Delete Account. You will be prompted to enter your password and then confirm the deletion of your account.

Using Foliage

How do I log a care activity?

You can either click on the Quick Log button on the bottom right corner or click on the Log Activity button on the plant's profile page and select the care activity you want to log. You can add notes, photos, and the date and time of the activity.

What can I set a reminder for?

You can set reminders for watering, repotting, fertilizing, misting, sowing, harvesting, blooming, applying pesticides, pruning, and caring if the plant is sick. You can also set your own custom reminder.

Technical and Troubleshooting

How do I contact support?

Click on the Contact button at the top of the page and fill out the form with your information and issue. We will get back to you as soon as possible.

Why is my plant not showing up in the search results?

Make sure you are spelling the plant's name correctly and that the plant is in our database. If you are still having trouble, contact support.
